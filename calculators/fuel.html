Calculate Your Trip Fuel Expenses

Rising fuel prices make trip planning more important than ever. Our Fuel Cost Calculator helps you estimate exactly how much you'll spend on gas for your next commute or road trip.

The Calculation Formula

We take your Distance, divide it by your vehicle's Fuel Efficiency (MPG or L/100km), and multiply by the current Fuel Price.

Tips to Save on Fuel

  • Efficient Driving: Smooth acceleration and consistent speeds improve MPG.
  • Vehicle Maintenance: Properly inflated tires and regular oil changes keep your car efficient.
  • Load Weight: Carrying excess weight inside the car increases fuel consumption.

Frequently Asked Questions
