Calculate Your Academic Success

Maintaining a high GPA is key to securing scholarships and university admissions. Our GPA Calculator follows standard credit-hour systems to help you track your progress throughout the semester.

How GPA is Weighted

Your Grade Point Average is not just an average of your grades. It is a Weighted Average based on the number of credit hours (or units) for each course. A 4-unit 'A' impacts your GPA more than a 2-unit 'A'.

Using the 4.0 or 5.0 Scale

  • Quality Points: Each grade (A, B, C, etc.) is assigned a point value.
  • Cumulative GPA: Track your entire academic history by adding previous totals.

Use this tool to simulate 'what-if' scenarios and see what grades you need in your finals to reach your target GPA.
