Calculate Your Daily Calorie Needs

Achieving your fitness goals starts with understanding your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE). Whether you want to lose weight, gain muscle, or maintain your current physique, caloric balance is the primary factor.

How Calories are Estimated

We use the Mifflin-St Jeor Equation, widely considered the most accurate formula for estimating Basel Metabolic Rate (BMR) for most people.

The Role of Activity Levels

  • Sedentary: Desk jobs and little exercise.
  • Moderate: Active lifestyle with 3-5 workouts per week.
  • Athlete: Intense training or physically demanding jobs.

Adjusting your activity level ensures your calorie target matches your actual energy output.
