How to Interpret Your BMI Result

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a simple numerical value based on your height and weight. It is used world-wide as a screening tool to determine if a person is in a healthy weight range or at risk for developing chronic health conditions.

The Importance of Weight Categorization

Maintaining a healthy weight is key to overall longevity. Our BMI calculator and gauge help you quickly visualize where you fall on the World Health Organization's (WHO) classification scale.

Limitations of BMI

Remember that BMI is only one part of your health picture. It doesn't distinguish between muscle and fat. If you are extremely muscular or an elite athlete, your BMI might suggest you are overweight when you are actually quite fit. Consider using our Body Fat Calculator or Ideal Weight Calculator for a secondary measurement.
