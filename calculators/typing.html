Frequently Asked Questions

WPM (Words Per Minute) is calculated by taking the total number of correctly typed characters divided by 5 (average word length), then divided by the elapsed time in minutes.

The average typing speed is around 40 WPM. Professionals can type 70-80 WPM, and advanced typists can reach over 100 WPM.
