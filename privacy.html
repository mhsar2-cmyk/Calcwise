Section 01

Overview

The short version: CalcWise does not collect, store, or sell your personal data. All calculations happen directly in your browser. We never see your numbers, inputs, or results.

This Privacy Policy describes how CalcWise ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you visit calcwises.com (the "Site"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being fully transparent about our data practices.

By using CalcWise, you agree to the terms described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Site.

  • Financial data, health data, or any calculation data

Section 03

How We Use Information

The limited technical data we collect is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Operating and maintaining the CalcWise website
  • Understanding which calculators are most useful to visitors
  • Improving site performance, speed, and user experience
  • Detecting and preventing fraudulent or abusive traffic
  • Complying with legal obligations
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our advertising (via Google AdSense)

We never sell your data. We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer any information about our visitors to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Section 04

Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by your browser. CalcWise uses cookies in the following ways:

Essential Cookies ‚Äî Required for the site to function. These cannot be disabled.

Analytics Cookies ‚Äî Google Analytics uses cookies to help us understand how visitors use the site. Data collected is aggregated and anonymous.

Advertising Cookies ‚Äî Google AdSense uses cookies to serve relevant advertisements. These cookies may track your browsing behavior across websites to show personalized ads.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

To opt out of Google's advertising cookies, visit google.com/settings/ads or install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Section 05

Advertising

CalcWise uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. This is how we keep the site free for everyone.

Google AdSense may use cookies and web beacons to collect data about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide relevant advertisements. The types of data Google may collect include:

CalcWise has no access to or control over cookies placed by Google AdSense. Google's privacy policy governs their data practices: policies.google.com/privacy

Section 07

Children's Privacy

CalcWise is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13 (or under 16 in the European Union).

Since we do not collect personal information from any users, we do not knowingly collect it from children either. Our calculators are educational tools suitable for all ages.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information to us, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove that information.

Section 09

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information associated with your use of CalcWise:

  • All traffic is encrypted via HTTPS / SSL (the padlock in your browser)
  • Calculator inputs are processed entirely in your browser and never transmitted
  • We do not store financial, health, or personal calculation data on any server
  • Our hosting provider (Vercel) maintains industry-standard security practices

While we implement these safeguards, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted to or from our Site.
