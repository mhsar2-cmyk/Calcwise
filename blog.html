The 10% Trick

To find any percentage: first find 10% (move the decimal point left), then multiply or add.

  • 15% tip on $64: 10% = $6.40, half of that = $3.20, total = $9.60
  • 20% tip on $85: 10% = $8.50 √ó 2 = $17.00
  • 25% off $120: 25% = quarter = $30. Sale price = $90

The "Rule of 5" for Multiplication

To multiply any number by 5: multiply by 10, then divide by 2. Example: 47 √ó 5 = 470 √∑ 2 = 235.
