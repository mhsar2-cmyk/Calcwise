Our Mission

What is CalcWise?

CalcWise is a free browser-based calculator platform featuring over 36 tools across finance, health, mathematics, and everyday life. Every calculation runs entirely inside your browser — we never see your numbers.

We built CalcWise because most online calculators are slow, cluttered with ads that block your results, or require you to sign up just to use basic tools. CalcWise is different: instant, clean, and completely free.

Available in both English and Arabic — one of the few calculator platforms designed specifically with Arabic-speaking users in mind, with full RTL support and localized units.

Privacy First

We built CalcWise on a simple principle: a calculator doesn't need to know your salary to calculate your tax. Your inputs are yours. Period.

When you type numbers into CalcWise, those numbers never leave your device. Everything runs in JavaScript, entirely in your browser — no server ever processes your mortgage inputs or your health data.

We do use Google Analytics to understand general traffic patterns and Google AdSense to serve ads that keep the site free. Read our full Privacy Policy for details.
