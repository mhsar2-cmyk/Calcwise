📐 Position Size Calculator

Results

Enter your trade details to calculate the optimal position size.

About this Tool

Our Position Size Calculator helps you manage risk by determining the exact amount of capital to allocate to a trade. Professional trading isn't just about where you enter, but how much you risk. By inputs your account balance, risk percentage, and stop loss, you ensure that no single trade can significantly damage your portfolio.

How to Use

  • Enter your current Account Balance in USD or your local currency.
  • Set your Risk per Trade percentage (typically 1-2% for pros).
  • Input your Entry Price and Stop Loss Price based on your technical analysis.
  • Click "Calculate" to see your maximum risk in dollars and the number of units to buy.

💰 Profit/Loss Calculator

Results

Enter trade details to calculate your potential profit or loss.

About this Tool

Calculate your potential profit or loss before you even place a trade. This tool supports both Long (buying for growth) and Short (profiting from decline) positions. It gives you a clear picture of your net return, including the percentage growth or decline of your investment.

How to Use

  • Enter your Entry Price (where you plan to buy or sell).
  • Enter your target Exit Price.
  • Input the Quantity or total units of the asset.
  • Select the Direction (Long for buys, Short for sells).
  • Click "Calculate P/L" to see your total profit/loss and ROI.
