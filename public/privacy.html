Privacy Policy 🛡️

Your data is yours. Learn how we protect it.

Last Updated: March 10, 2026

1. Introduction

CalcWise ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

We collect minimal information to provide you with the best experience:

  • Registration Data: Name and email address when you create an account.

3. Use of Information

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain our services.

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

We use cookies to remember your theme and language preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function correctly.

Our site contains links to other services like TradingView. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party sites.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@calcwise.com.
